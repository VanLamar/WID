Welcome to the Wiki

Welcome to the WID Wiki, your authoritative source for everything related to the world of Whispers in Darkness. Our pages are full of detailed guides, tips and tools to help you explore, modify and enjoy the game to the fullest.

Whispers in Darkness is an exciting adventure where you play as a child who has to face mysterious creatures every night. Explore the game's world by day, solve puzzles, interact with NPCs and influence your nighttime adventures. Your choice of dialogs and actions can change the course of the story and your relationship with the characters. So be vigilant, everyone can take offense.
